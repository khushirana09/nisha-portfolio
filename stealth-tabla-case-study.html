The Challenge

Tabla Indian Restaurant, a fast-growing restaurant chain in Orlando, wanted to boost its online presence, rank higher in local search results, and increase online reservations across multiple locations. While the brand had strong offline traction, its digital visibility was fragmented, with unstructured content, under-optimized location pages, and low blog engagement.

My Role

As the SEO Content Writer under Stealth Management Group, I led content-focused SEO efforts to improve visibility, engagement, and conversion across their digital assets.

What I Did

1. Website Content Architecture & Optimization

  • Planned a user-friendly navigation structure across multiple location pages.
  • Wrote SEO-optimized content for service, about, and menu pages.
  • Implemented on-page SEO best practices (title tags, meta descriptions, internal linking).
  • Created clear calls-to-action for reservations and contact.

2. Geo-Targeted Location Pages

  • Crafted dedicated content for each location (Orlando, Winter Park, Lake Nona, Oviedo).
  • Included keywords, Google Map embeds, location-specific FAQs, and CTAs.
